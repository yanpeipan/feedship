## Decisions

1. **InsightChain splits clusters by article count**: Rich clusters (>= 2 articles) get full topic/insight generation; simple clusters (< 2 articles) get simple one-sentence summary via separate lightweight chain
2. **Topic ID normalization**: LLM outputs "Topic 1", "Topic 2" format; InsightChain normalizes to "Topic_01", "Topic_02"
3. **cluster.summary from first topic**: For rich clusters, cluster.summary is set to the first topic's summary
