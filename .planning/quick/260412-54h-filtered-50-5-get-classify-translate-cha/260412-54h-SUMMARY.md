# Phase quick-260412-54h Plan 01 Summary

## One-liner

Batched concurrent LLM calls to `get_classify_translate_chain` with 50 articles/batch and 5 concurrent batches.

## What Was Done

Implemented batched, concurrent processing of articles through the classify+translate LLM chain:

1. **Split filtered into batches of 50** articles each
2. **Used `asyncio.Semaphore(5)`** to limit to 5 concurrent LLM calls
3. **Per-batch `news_list` construction** passed to `get_classify_translate_chain(tag_list=tag_list, news_list=news_list, target_lang=target_lang)`
4. **ID adjustment with `batch_offset`** to maintain global ID continuity across batches
5. **Flattened results** into a single `ClassifyTranslateOutput` for downstream processing

## Implementation Details

The `process_batch` function:
- Takes a batch of articles, its offset, and the semaphore
- Builds `news_list` from the batch (1-indexed within batch)
- Creates chain with batch's `news_list` passed to constructor
- Invokes chain with `ainvoke({})`
- Adjusts returned item IDs by adding `batch_offset`
- Returns list of `ClassifyTranslateItem`

`asyncio.gather(*tasks)` processes all batches concurrently, semaphore limits to 5 at a time.
